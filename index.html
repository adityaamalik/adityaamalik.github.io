ABOUT

ABOUT MIET

Meerut Institute of Engineering and Technology (MIET), located in the National Capital Region (NCR), is one of the best and oldest self-financed institutes in Uttar Pradesh, India. MIET is NAAC Accredited. This Institute today is a leading Institute known for it's academic excellence and placement at MIET special emphasis has been placed on developing highly conductive environment for building of a solid foundation of knowledge, personality development, confidence building, pursuit of excellence and self-discipline which helps to produce professional well trained for the rigors of professional and social life. It lays claim to have an extensive academic environment, finest faculties and best infrastructure to provide quality education.

ABOUT DEPARTMENT OF PHARMACEUTICAL TECHNOLOGY, MIET

The department of Pharmaceutical Technology at MIET, Meerut is one of the premier department in Pharmacy Education & Research in Uttar Pradesh. India. The department was established to be "Center of Excellence" by imparting quality education & developing vision & research skills in young Pharmacy Professional. The Department of Pharmaceutical Technology is Accredited by the NBA & also an Approved Research Center of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow, for conducting a Ph.D. Program. The Department is well equipped with "State-of-the-art" laboratories. The Department faculty is highly qualified, experienced & specialized in different areas of Pharmacy.

ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

The Conference intends to promote high standards in the Pharmacy profession by way of extending opportunities to the academicians and working professionals by providing a forum for sharing their knowledge, experiences, innovations, and inventions. The Conference cum poster presentation session will provide an excellent opportunity to Scientists, Technologists, Educationists, Researchers, and Students to come on a common platform to discuss and the exchange ideas on recent development in the field of Pharmaceutical Sciences and facilitate exchange of new ideas among members of the Pharmacy Profession.
